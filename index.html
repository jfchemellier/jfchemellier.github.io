Biography

Jean-François Petit de Chemellier is a Chinese to French translator and teaches translation at Tamkang University (Taiwan). He holds a PhD in Translation and Interpretation from National Taiwan Normal University. His research interests include linguistic relativity, translation theory, and neurolinguistics.
